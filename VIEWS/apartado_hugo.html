My name is Hugo Cano

Hi, I am 18 years old and I was born in the state of Tabasco on September 30, 2006.

In this presentation, I am going to talk to you about my story, from my childhood to the moment when I am studying a degree in Cancun.

Take your snacks and listen carefully.

My childhood

My childhood was quiet, I neither lived with luxuries nor eccentricities. Since I was little, my parents taught me to value the things I have, but above all, the moments with the important people.

I lived in Tabasco until I was 9 years old, and I have been living in Cancun for 9 years now. So that's half of my life.
